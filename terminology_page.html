Map Callouts

Map: Bind

Bind is a two site map where attackers are infiltrating from a beach coast. This map has two sites and no middle: a side, left or right, must be picked. Instead of having a middle, the sites are binded together through two one-way teleporters which allow players to quickly rotate or flank the enemy team. This map is inspired but the coordinates at 34°2'A" N 6°51'Z" W, off the coast of Rabat, Morocco. The lore of this map revolve around being Cypher, one of the agents' home.

Map: Ascent

Ascent was the first map added since Valorant's official release. It is a two-site map with the most open areas and a large courtyard in the center of the map. This courtyard is a quick shortcut between sights, but is risky as it does not offer much cover. This map has closeable doors, which was the first of its kind to enter the game. This map is based off of the coordinates at 45°26'BF" N 12°20'Q" E in San Marco, Venice, Italy and contains many landmarks from the area.

Map: Icebox

Icebox is a map that takes place in a tunfra climate. It is one of the most complicated maps as it has many angles and cornerers as well as verticality to cover. It is a two-site map with a two-tier middle. This map is great for certain agents such as Raze, Jett and Omen who are able to reach the high ground. It is based off of the coordinates at 76°44'A" N 149°30'Z" E, off the coast of Bennett Island, Sakha, Russia. THe lore behind this map lies within the stolen mask behind the shattered glass in the A site, which is used in agent Yoru's ultimate. It is believed that Yoru carried out the break-in.

Map: Haven

Haven is unique because it is the only map with three bomb sites. One site is meant for long range combat while another is tighter quarters and will engage in close combat. This map is inspired by the coordinates at 27°28'A" N 89°38'WZ" E, in Thimphu, Bhutan.

Map: Breeze

Breeze is a map that has a tropical island setting and is one of the largest maps in size. It is a two site map with a large, open middle. It features two tiers in certain places and is based on the coordinates at 26°11'AG" N 71°10'WY" W, off the coast of the Bahamas, in the middle of the Bermuda Triangle, Atlantic Ocean. It is the lore of this map that agent KAY/O arrived on earth through the teleporter on this map as it was abandonned and inactive and had a sign on it saying "sequence initiated", which is one of the agent's voicelines.

Map: Split

Split is a map that is named the way it is because the left side of the map is split from the right side through how poor it is. This map will show dirt roads and half destroyed houses on the left-hand side, and on the right-hand side you will find billboards, fancy homes and offices, etc. The map is literally split in a war of poor v rich. This map has two sites and a middle area that provides high ground dominance. This map is inspired by the coordinates at 35°41'CD" N 139°41'WX" E, in Shinjuku, Tokyo, Japan.

Map: Fracture

Fracture is a top-secret experiment site split apart by a radianite-fueled disaster. In its simplest form, the map is marked by its H-shape layout with a visual split down the middle that reveals contrasting sides. Long ziplines also connect routes underneath. This map has heavy and interactive lore tied to it and is based on the coordinates at 35°48'BI" N 106°08'YQ" W, in Santa Fe, New Mexico, USA.
